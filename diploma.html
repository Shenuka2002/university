DEPLOMA LEVEL



Diploma In Human Resource Management
The Diploma in Human Resource Management is an integral part of our comprehensive business education programmes. This specialized program is designed to equip students with the essential knowledge and skills required for success in the dynamic field of Human Resource Management, while still providing a strong foundation in various aspects of business.
Diploma In Information Technology
The Level 3 Diploma in Information Technology is meticulously crafted to provide comprehensive theoretical and practical knowledge essential for beginners and mature students alike. This course establishes a robust foundation in the fundamentals of IT, serving as a springboard for future career endeavors.
Diploma in Network Engineering
The main objective of this study programme is to provide a systematic Software Engineering Course with the aim of producing qualified Software Engineers and IT personnel for the Sri Lankan job market. It is intention to ensure local as well as international recognition for this programme.
Diploma In English
This course is intended to develop the Listening, Speaking, Reading, and Writing Skills of students. The course will use interactive sessions and technologies based on the ‘Communicative Approach to Language Teaching’, which is the latest language teaching methodology practiced around the world.
